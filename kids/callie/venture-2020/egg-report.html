Callie's egg drop experiment

Basket 1: A pop bottle

Figure 1: A pop bottle with a large bag as parachute

Figure 2: Using a smaller part of a bag as parachute

My pop bottle basket did not work well. I tried two different parachutes. I used a hard boiled egg.

Basket 2: An egg carton

Figure 3: An egg carton with the smaller parachute; the egg survived!

Figure 4: The second time the egg broke

My egg carton basket with a parachute worked okay. I used a hard boiled egg. The egg broke on the second drop. The best part of it was the egg was snug.

Basket 3: A tupperware container

Figure 5: The uncooked egg inside this container survived! :)

My tupperware basket worked well. It did not have a parachute. Instead I filled it with shredded paper and cotton balls. The egg was uncooked. The egg didn't break! The best part of it was it was soft.
